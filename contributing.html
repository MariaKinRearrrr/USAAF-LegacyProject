🛩 Contributing to the Legacy

"Beneath the clouds they fought, beyond the stars they inspire."

We are a non-profit volunteer group based in Thailand dedicated to gathering, preserving, and archiving the words, comforting messages, morale-boosting encouragement, and inspiring speeches of the U.S. Army Air Forces (USAAF) personnel during World War II.

Our mission does not limit itself to the renowned historical figures or well-known war heroes. We firmly believe that every voice from the sky matters—whether they were celebrated or forgotten by time. Through this archive, we aim to pass down their profound legacy to future generations, hoping these words will serve as a timeless source of daily inspiration and courage to face life's challenges.

📬 How You Can Help

If you possess any historical data, authentic quotes, personal letters, pilot biographies, or any relative records regarding the USAAF that you have discovered, we would be deeply honored to feature them in our archive.
